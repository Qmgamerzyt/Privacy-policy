Privacy Policy

Welcome to Lumora's Privacy Policy. We care deeply about your privacy and this policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

1. What We Collect

Lumora may collect the following information when you interact with the bot:

  • Server IDs where the bot is added
  • User IDs for moderation logs or custom features
  • Message content (only if a command requires it, never stored permanently)

2. How We Use Data

We only use data to provide bot features like moderation, logging, and server customization. None of your data is sold, shared, or used for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage

We store minimal necessary data in a secure MongoDB database. Data like server settings or preferences may be stored to personalize your experience.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share any personal information with third-party services or individuals. Your data stays strictly between your server and our bot.

5. Cookies & Web Tracking

This site does not use cookies or any tracking scripts. It's kept simple and respectful of your privacy.
